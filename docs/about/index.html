About this blog

Whilst I give every impression that this blog is all of my own work, I must mention that I am indebted to Steve Holt who could be said to be as much the author as I am! He is the only "real" person I know and he has been my right hand throughout the time I have written this blog. He is also something of a technical genius, having created for me a way of writing this blog in ordinary text and transforming it using Node, Metalsmith (whatever they are) and Handlebars (probably nothing to do with bicycles) into what you see - white hot!

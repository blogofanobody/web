Gathering the news

4th of April 2018 - Sources disclosed

I have been a bit rushed today and am suffering from a lack of newspapers. I tried to watch the news on television last night but found that our aerial is quite inadequate, and whilst I could see what was happening, we had no sound. In the case of political debate that is a severe limitation. So I have had to rely on the BBC website today. I do not intend to make it a habit, my preference being for printed media.
